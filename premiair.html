Meeting Recap: Full Demo

Solving the "Blind Engineer" Problem

Attendees: Chris Harrington (Premiair), Oliver Abrahamson (BigChange).

Premiair is a national leader in AC and building services, but your current system is "split up and all over the place." Key pain points:

  • Zero History on App: Engineers can't see what was done previously on a job without calling the office or waiting for a WhatsApp of a report.
  • Manual Asset Tracking: Tracking 100s of AC units manually is a "hard thing to monitor," leading to missed service revenue.
  • Fragmented Certifications: Using separate apps (like GasApp) alongside Paperdrop creates a data silo for compliance.
  • Hidden Admin Costs: Significant time spent by 8 office staff manually processing job sheets and chasing engineers.

Current State vs. Future State

Moving from manual math and phone calls to enterprise-grade automation.

Current State (Paperdrop & Manual)

  • Phone Call Dependency: Engineers frequently call back to the office for job history/reports.
  • "Missed Jobs": Reminding clients of AC services is manual; revenue is likely slipping through the cracks.
  • Disconnected Systems: Finance, gas certs, and scheduling aren't talking to each other.
  • Intercompany Friction: Messy intercompany charges when engineers work across different entities.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Full Field Visibility: Engineers see 100% of site history, photos, and past reports instantly on the app.
  • AI Smart Summary: Hot-off-the-press AI summaries give engineers a 30-second snapshot of site health on arrival.
  • Automated Service Reminders: System alerts clients automatically when assets are due, driving order book growth.
  • Unified CRM/Finance: One source of truth from quote to job to Xero invoice.

The Business Case for Premiair Group

Focusing on the "Hidden Costs" of current inefficiencies.

Revenue Preservation

Service Automation: By automating reminders for AC service due dates, you eliminate the "missed job" scenario Chris identified, turning a customer service win into a revenue win.

Upsell Insight: AI-powered site summaries allow engineers to identify aged assets and quote for replacement while still on site.

Asset Intelligence

The AC Database: Move from "trying to track" to full Asset DNA. Every serial number, gas log, and repair history is linked to the specific unit at Wembley or any other site.

Compliance Vault: Gas safe and F-Gas logs are produced natively—no more separate GasApp subscriptions or manual paper filing.

Operational Excellence

  • Mandatory Risk Assessments: Force dynamic risk assessments before the "Start Job" button is unlocked.
  • Intercompany Logic: Use BigChange folders to manage different entities while keeping the office view unified.
  • Xero Sync: Two-way sync ensures that once an invoice is paid in Xero, it's marked as paid in BigChange.

Success Metrics for Premiair Group

Defining what "Done" looks like for your digital transformation.

Efficiency Gains

  • Call Volume Reduction: Aim for a 60% reduction in "history request" phone calls from engineers.
  • Report Speed: Job reports available to clients 0 seconds after job completion.

Financial Clarity

  • Margin Visibility: Real-time job costing showing 97% vs 50% margin jobs instantly.
  • Invoice Velocity: Reduce time from "Job Done" to "Invoice Sent" from days to minutes.
