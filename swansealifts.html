Meeting 1: System Demo

Escaping Legacy Constraints

Key Contacts: Rebecca, Stuart, Serge, Oli.

Swansea & Keighley Lifts run a complex multi-site operation with 10 engineers, currently managed through a combination of "Service Life" (legacy server software), Excel, and manual paper trails.

  • Legacy Bottleneck: Service Life is server-based, making remote access and modern field workflows difficult.
  • The Paper Trail: Manual job sheets in Keighley and mailed lists in Swansea lead to data lag and potential loss.
  • Financial Friction: Manual checking of invoices against job sheets is time-consuming and prone to human error.
  • Compliance Burden: Forcing vehicle checks and dynamic risk assessments is currently "debatable" due to lack of system enforcement.

Legacy vs. BigChange

Visualising the transition to a modern field service environment.

Current State (Manual & Disjointed)

  • Fragmented Systems: Service Life, RAM Tracking, and Quickbooks don't talk to each other.
  • Manual Math: Office staff manually verify hours on site against job sheets for invoicing.
  • Field Lag: Engineers in Swansea receive paper lists, with photos sent back via WhatsApp.
  • Compliance Risks: No forced "No-Access" or "Risk Assessment" workflows on site.

Future State (BigChange)

  • All-in-One Cloud: CRM, Tracking, Jobs, and Invoicing in one login—access from anywhere.
  • Automated Billing: Jobs marked "Complete" on the app flow straight into Quickbooks.
  • Digital Job Sheets: Live updates from Wales to Keighley the moment a job is signed.
  • Enforced Compliance: Mandatory vehicle checks and site risk assessments before jobs start.

The Business Case

Unlocking value through automation and real-time visibility.

Operational Efficiency

Route Optimization: Schedule jobs based on live locations, reducing drive time for the 10 engineers across Wales and Yorkshire.

Live Progress: Eliminate "What's happening on site?" calls with real-time job status updates.

Financial Control

Eliminate Leakage: Automatically track parts used on jobs to ensure every component is billed to the client.

Quickbooks Sync: Export invoice-ready files instantly, saving hours of office data entry.

Compliance & Safety

Forced Workflows: Ensure every site has a completed LOLER/Service checklist and photo evidence before completion.

Fleet Management: Automated MOT/Service alerts and daily vehicle health checks via the app.
