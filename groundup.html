Escaping Software Limitations

Attendees: Arianna Molinari, Shiva Sharma, Oliver Abrahamson (BigChange), Roger Quinn (BigChange).

Ground Up is hitting a "bespoke bottleneck" where existing tools are slowing your operation down. Key challenges discussed:

  • E-works Limitations: The system is too rigid; you can't customize templates or make fields mandatory without requesting dev changes.
  • Tracking Blindspots: Ariana currently has to call every driver to find their location or check van compliance.
  • Compliance Headaches: Shiva is currently trying to build manual workarounds for risk assessments that should be native.
  • Finance Friction: Xero sync issues are resulting in blank invoices and manual reconciliation.

Current State vs. Future State

Transitioning from manual check-ins to real-time enterprise visibility.

Current State (Manual & Disjointed)

  • "Call Everyone" Management: Hours wasted weekly by office staff calling drivers to locate vans or check stock.
  • Static Reporting: Templates are fixed; you can't add the specific keywords or data Ground Up needs.
  • Manual Compliance: Using external Microsoft Forms for vehicle checks, creating data silos.
  • Sync Errors: No real-time visibility on whether an invoice successfully hit Xero or failed.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Live Map Visibility: Ariana sees all 10 vans in real-time, reducing calls and increasing fleet efficiency.
  • Unlimited Customization: Use the Word Plugin to build the exact reports Ground Up requires with no "requests" needed.
  • Integrated Workflows: Mandatory risk assessments and vehicle checks completed directly on the app.
  • Seamless Xero Sync: Real-time sync status indicators and automatic reconciliation of payments.
