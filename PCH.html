Discovery & Goals

Recapping the core objectives for Environmental Services.

Discovery: Feb 10, 2026

Unifying the "Biggest Department"

Stakeholders: Andy Miller, Sandra Fear, Dave Ryland, Joe Berryman, Paul Hutchings.

The goal is to move Environmental Services (Grounds, Caretaking) off disjointed Excel sheets and paper. Key challenges identified:

  • Manual Workflow Gap: Work is still handed out on "bits of paper" every morning, creating a massive administrative burden.
  • Manual Math Bottleneck: Management and IT spend excessive time manually calculating KPIs and responding to tenant queries.
  • Lone Worker Safety: Need for integrated GPS tracking for high-risk reactive works and mobile operatives across the city.
  • Resident Transparency: A vision to surface "last cut" data into the tenant portal via API.

Current State vs. Future State

Modernizing the tools for PCH's frontline teams.

Current State (Paper & Excel)

  • Reactive Friction: Emergency maintenance or fly-tipping requires manual coordination and manual input.
  • Data Re-Entry: Staff spend hours daily moving data from paper sheets into spreadsheets for reporting.
  • Limited Visibility: Difficult to track city-wide ride-on mower progress in real-time.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Live Field Reports: Thumbs up/down completion with photos, instantly visible to Ops Managers.
  • Automated Scheduling: Route optimization for grounds teams to minimize fuel and maximize site time.
  • Dynamic Site History: Every communal area and green space has a digital history—visible on site via mobile maps.

The Business Case for PCH

Driving efficiency across 2,000+ sites and 70,000+ residents.

Operational Efficiency

Route Optimization: Maximize the impact of the ride-on mower and mobile teams by clustering jobs geographically, reducing "dead time" across Plymouth.

Grounds Management

Site-Based Tracking: Field teams can log completions directly against site records, providing a clear digital trail of all grounds maintenance activities.

Admin & IT Recovery

API-First Integration: Power BI or Snowflake integration allows Sandra and Paul to surface KPIs without manual data cleansing.

Success Metrics for PCH

How we will measure the "BigChange" in Environmental Services.

Field Productivity

  • 15% Capacity Gain: Reducing travel and admin time allows teams to cover more sites per week.
  • Real-time Proof: 100% photo evidence for every grass cut and communal clean.

Safety & Compliance

  • Lone Worker Compliance: Integrated check-ins and GPS breadcrumbs for staff working in isolated areas.
  • Audit Readiness: Digital waste transfer notes for every fly-tip removal, stored for 7 years.
