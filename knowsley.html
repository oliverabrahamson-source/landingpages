Key Return on Investment (ROI)

Based on our discussion, BigChange directly addresses your key efficiency challenges to increase profitability and consistency.

Automated Repeat Callout Warnings

Eliminate missed problem jobs. The system provides mandatory, visual pop-up warnings (instead of ignorable fields) when logging a repeat call to a site within a specific time frame (e.g., 30 days).

Sage Live Sync & Rate Cards

Achieve live two-way synchronisation with Sage 50. Automated job-to-invoice generation using precise, pre-defined client rate cards (e.g., standard, 5-12, weekend rates), ensuring accurate and fast billing.

Auto-Reporting & Client Messaging

Save hours of office admin by **automatically sending digital job reports** (PDF) post-completion. Also automates job confirmations and 'on the way' messages to clients, drastically reducing inbound/outbound phone calls.

Key Feature: Granular Asset & Job History

Schedule directly against a specific lift serial number (asset). Instantly pull up all maintenance, call-outs, and inspection history against that individual asset, crucial for detailed reporting and client accountability.
