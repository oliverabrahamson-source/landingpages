Resource & Scheduling Strategy

Managing shift patterns and dedicated engineer routes.

Shift Groups

Resources are grouped as NIGHT (by Trade) or DAY to allow rapid filtering during emergency outages.

Fixed Allocations

Specific recurring jobs (e.g. LEE @ HARRODS) are hard-linked to specific engineers on set days of the week.

Bulk Rescheduling

A "Yesterday" catch-all view allows the planning team to bulk-move incomplete works from the prior day at 8:00 AM.

PPM & Asset Efficiency

Simplifying maintenance cycles for high-asset sites.

The Multi-Asset Approach

Rather than cluttering the board with "one job per asset," Barden FM will utilize Multi-Asset Job Sheets. A single visit covers all site equipment, with individual asset checklist results stored within the parent job.
